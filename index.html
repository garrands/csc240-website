Welcome to Troop 4054!

Since 1969, Troop 4054 B has been serving the youth of the Town of the Town of Ballston, NY.
We are chartered by American Legion Post #1817, and are a member of the Mohawk District in the Twin Rivers Council.

Troop meetings are held Mondays from 645PM to 800PM at:
Our Lady of Grace Church, 73 Midline Rd., Ballston Lake, NY 12019

Interested in joining?

We are always looking to welcome new members attend volunteers into our troop. Please contact us and one of our troop leaders will get back to you soon.
